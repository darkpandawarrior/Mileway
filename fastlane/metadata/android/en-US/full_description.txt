Mileway is an offline-first mileage and expense tracker.

- Track trips with GPS. It works fully offline, with no account required.
- Log mileage and expenses; everything is stored locally on your device.
- Review saved trips, export your data, and manage reimbursement vouchers.

The F-Droid (FOSS) build uses open-source maps (MapLibre) and contains no
proprietary Google or Firebase components.
